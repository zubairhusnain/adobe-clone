Frequently asked questions

What does Creative Cloud for teams include?

Creative Cloud for teams provides businesses of all sizes access to 20+ creative apps including Photoshop, Illustrator, and Acrobat Pro, as well as tools for simplified administration and IP protection.

All plans include 1TB of cloud storage per user, enabling team members to easily share files, view company assets, and collaborate effectively through features like shared libraries, link sharing, and co-editing.

How is Creative Cloud for teams different from Creative Cloud for individuals and enterprise?

Creative Cloud for teams is designed for businesses with enhanced collaboration and control. It includes everything in our Creative Cloud for individuals plans but does not include the advanced security, license management, and support of enterprise plans.

Creative Cloud for teams plans include:

  • Pooled storage: 1TB per user, shared across the team
  • Shared resources: Company-owned libraries accessible to all
  • Extended history: 180-day version histories for cloud documents
  • Asset protection: Ability to reclaim assets from departing team members
  • Simplified management: Web-based admin console and centralized IT tools
  • Dedicated support: 24/7 technical support and expert 1:1 sessions
  • Complimentary membership: All company employees get access to Adobe Express, Acrobat Online Services, and ability to view company files, custom fonts, and more
  • Talent acquisition: Unlimited job posts on Behance

The enterprise plan adds exclusive generative AI features, centralized deployment and access controls, single sign-on (SSO), and unlimited support from Adobe product experts.

How many users can use a single license?
Each license is assigned to a single user on a team. That user can sign in to Creative Cloud apps on up to two computers at a time.
Are there Creative Cloud training resources available for our team?
Yes, Adobe is invested in helping your team successfully create high-quality content. You can access many hands-on tutorials, videos, and articles on Adobe Learn.
Members of my organization already have individual Creative Cloud memberships. Can I switch them to Creative Cloud for teams?
Yes, team members with individual Creative Cloud memberships can upgrade to Creative Cloud for teams with no cancellation fee. To do so, call {{phone-number-cct}} or use chat for support.
Can I use Creative Cloud for teams on multiple computers?
Your Creative Cloud for teams subscription lets you activate each of the 20+ Creative Cloud apps across desktop, mobile, and web on up to two devices (or virtual machines) at a time, but you may not use the same app on both devices simultaneously.
What's the best AI photo editor for small businesses?

Part of Creative Cloud for teams, Photoshop is the industry standard for precision, AI-powered image editing, making it one of the best AI photo editors to help small businesses create images faster. With Photoshop, teams can:

  • Upscale images with Topaz Labs .
  • Make seamless composites with Harmonize.
  • Remove image backgrounds and add or modify content with Generative Fill using leading AI models from Adobe, Google, OpenAI, and more.
What's the best AI video generator for small businesses?
With Creative Cloud for teams, small businesses have access to Firefly — a great option for text-to-video generation. The Firefly AI Video Generator lets teams instantly create high-quality video clips for social media or digital marketing, saving time and money. Learn more.
